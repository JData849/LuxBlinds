About Grove

Crafted elegance, delivered with care.

At Grove, we believe that nothing transforms a space quite like premium shutters. More than a window treatment, shutters are architectural features - defining light, enhancing privacy, and elevating interiors with timeless elegance.

With over 25 years of experience, we specialise in crafting beautiful, made-to-measure plantation shutters that fuse function with refined design. From traditional sash frames to minimalist modern spaces, shutters are the heart of our offering - durable, stylish, and endlessly customisable.

As a proudly family-run business, Grove was founded on trust, craftsmanship, and exceptional service. We don't operate a showroom. Instead, we bring the full design experience to you - helping you envision the perfect finish within the context of your own space and natural light.

While we also offer a curated range of bespoke blinds, shutters are our signature: the gold standard for homes seeking both beauty and performance.

Every Grove project is personally overseen by our founders, ensuring your journey is smooth, collaborative, and built around your vision.
